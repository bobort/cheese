We offer lectures, tutoring, and group sessions to help you improve your scores on your medical exams. We study at Lodgic Everyday Community in Champaign, Illinois, so if you'd like to participate in person, you will need to pay a membership fee to Lodgic in order to join us. Lodgic does have child care available for purchase. However, we are also available online without any extra fees! Our standard online meeting tool is Zoom.

You are responsible for purchasing your own question bank and practice exams, but please talk to us first to get advice on what you should purchase when you should purchase it.

Lectures Formal Learning Sessions

Lectures give you the opportunity to learn material in a formal fashion. Each lecture is chock-full of information that will help you succeed in passing your exam and improve your knowledge for use in the field.

Includes:

  • Recorded lectures and live lectures
  • Two sessions of tutoring each week
  • Group sessions
  • Counseling, support, and follow-up emails are always included

You are responsible for paying for your practice exams and question bank separately. You are welcome to attend live sessions at Lodgic Everyday Community. You are responsible for paying for membership fees to Lodgic Everyday Community; however, all sessions are available online at no extra cost.

Tutoring One-on-one Sessions

Every time we work with you in a one-on-one tutoring session, you will feel better about your career path, and you will have a plan of action that will improve your score.

  • Available in-person at Lodgic Everyday Community in Champaign, Illinois OR online using Zoom
  • Buy enough tutoring sessions at once to get free access to the group sessions
  • Two tutoring sessions each week are included in the course purchase
  • Counseling, support, and follow-up emails are always included

Group Sessions Learning and Teaching with Peers

Improve your knowledge and conceptual retention by studying with your peers. With several different formats available for group sessions, you will improve your scores.

Includes:

  • Morning Cute - Wake up early to review morning questions about specific topics
  • Drill Session - Become involved in explaining answers to questions
  • 707 - Explain a concept to your peers or listen to your peers explain a concept
  • Concepts for Lunch - Learn important medical concepts
